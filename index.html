About me

Hi! I am Reniel


Front-End

Developer

I am an amateur Front-end Developer & UI Developer. Focused on creating things with interactions and animations. With a knack for turning problems and opportunities into user-driven strategic solutions with delightful and intuitive interface design.


I enjoy spending time fixing minor details and learning new things about web development, particularly front-end and UI development, in order to improve my skills and knowledge. Also I like working in a team, you'll learn faster and much more. “As the saying goes, two heads are better than one.


Sometimes I make mistakes, but those mistakes teach me how to make it better next time.

Why me?

I am a fast learner and am willing to learn more for the sake of the company. My goal is to help small and medium-sized companies to grow their audience. I can make your website responsive. I can make a website that meets your standards with quality and clean code.


Moreover, I am a creative problem solver who is always up for a challenge. I am a quick learner who is always eager to keep up with the latest trends and technologies in the world of web development. I am also an excellent communicator who is able to work well in a team environment.


I am a highly motivated individual who is passionate about web development. I take pride in my work and I am always striving to improve my skills and knowledge. I have a strong work ethic and I am able to work under pressure and meet deadlines.


I believe that my skills, experience, and passion for web development make me a valuable asset to your organization. I am confident that I would be able to make a significant contribution to your team, and I would welcome the opportunity to discuss my qualifications in more detail.
